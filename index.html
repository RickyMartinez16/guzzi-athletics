About our trainer

Nick Guzzi

Head Trainer

Nick Guzzi is the head trainer at Guzzi Athletics, leading various programs aimed at enhancing athlete performance. A former pitcher at St. John's University and Stetson University, Nick made significant contributions during his freshman and sophomore seasons. Prior to college, he was a standout at Point Pleasant Borough High School, earning First Team All-State honors and ranking as the no. 7 recruit in New Jersey. Known for his academic achievements and athletic prowess, Nick combines his passion for baseball with expertise in training methodologies to help athletes reach their full potential.
